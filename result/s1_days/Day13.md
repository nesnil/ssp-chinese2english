### Day13

1. 你和你的新同学们相处得怎么样？（classmate）
  - How do you get along with your new classmates?
2. 你爬得越高，就会看得越远。（climb）
  - The higher you climb, the farther you will see.
3. 所有社团成员在这本相册里都有自己的照片。（club）
  - All the club members have their own pictures in this album.
4. 这款软件收集数据并对其进行分析。（collect）
  - This software collects data and analyzes it.
5. 互联网使世界各地的人能相互联系。（connect）
  - The Internet allows people around the world to connect with each other.
