### Day7

1. 信不信由你，那个魔术师正漂浮在空中！（believe）
  - Believe it or not, that magician is floating in the air!
2. 你最好不要关着灯看手机。（better）
  - You'd better not look at your phone with the light off.
3. 我的小狗从来不咬人或乱叫。（bark）
  - My puppy never bites or barks.
4. 把水烧开，给客人泡茶。（boil）
  - Boil the water and make tea for the guests.
5. 我建议你提前订好机票。（book）
  - I suggest you book the plane ticket in advance.
