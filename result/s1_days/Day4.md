### Day4

1. 这个动物几乎什么都吃。（almost）
  - This animal eats almost everything.
2. 一个士兵正沿着街道行走。（along）
  - A soldier is walking along the street.
3. 他已经完成了今天的家庭作业。（already）
  - He has already finished today's homework.
4. 我每天早上7点钟到学校。（arrive）
  - I arrive at school at 7 o'clock every morning.
5. 毕加索（Picasso）常被描述为20世纪最伟大的艺术家之一。（artist）
  - Picasso is often described as one of the greatest artists of the 20th century.
