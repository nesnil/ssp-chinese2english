### Day55

1. 这就是它如此特别的原因。（reason）
  - That's the reason why it is so special.
2. 每个孩子都有接受教育的权利。（receive）
  - Every child has the right to receive an education.
3. 他一进房间我就认出他了。（recognise）
  - I recognised him as soon as he came into the room.
4. 新年是人们走亲访友的好时候。（relative）
  - New Year is a good time for people to visit relatives and friends.
5. 玩游戏时牢记规则很重要。（remember）
  - It's important to remember the rules when playing games.
