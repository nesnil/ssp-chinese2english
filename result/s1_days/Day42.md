### Day42

1. 你需要在工作和个人生活之间取得更好的平衡。（personal）
  - You need to achieve a better balance between work and personal life.
2. 请把披萨分成六块。（pizza）
  - Divide the pizza into six pieces, please.
3. 去年夏天，我们在乡村度过了一次愉快的旅程。（pleasant）
  - We had a pleasant journey to the countryside last summer.
4. 嘴里塞满东西说话是不礼貌的。（polite）
  - It's not polite to talk with your mouth full.
5. 学校张贴了海报宣布这一重大消息。（poster）
  - The school put up posters to announce the big news.
