### Day37

1. 他是一个懒惰的学生，从不按时完成作业。（lazy）
  - He is a lazy student who never completes his homework on time.
2. 太空中其他行星上有生命吗？（life）
  - Is there life on other planets in space?
3. 列出你最喜欢的十首歌曲。（list）
  - Make a list of your ten favourite songs.
4. 他虽然是外国人，却非常了解当地的风俗。（local）
  - Although he is a foreigner, he knows the local custom very well.
5. 邻居家的音乐太大声了。（loud）
  - The music from the neighbour's house is too loud.
