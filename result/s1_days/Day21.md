### Day21

1. 出口在走廊的尽头。（end）
  - The exit is at the end of the hallway.
2. 游客们能享用来自世界各地的美食。（enjoy）
  - Tourists can enjoy delicious food from all around the world.
3. 科学家们需要足够的钱来继续他们的研究。（enough）
  - The scientists need enough money to continue their research.
4. 演出开始时所有人都安静下来了。（everyone）
  - Everyone became quiet when the show started.
5. 雷锋无私奉献的故事为我们树立了好榜样。（example）
  - Lei Feng's stories of selfless dedication set good examples for us.
