### Day58

1. 巴黎的埃菲尔（Eiffel）铁塔于1889年建成。（tower）
  - The Eiffel Tower in Paris was completed in 1889.
2. 是否能梦想成真取决于你有多努力。（true）
  - Whether your dream can come true or not depends on how hard you work.
3. 在任何关系中，都需要时间来建立信任。（trust）
  - It takes time to build trust in any relationship.
4. 我花了一会儿时间才理解这些指示。（understand）
  - It took me a while to understand the instructions.
5. 老人虚弱地几乎无法行走。（weak）
  - The old man was so weak that he could hardly walk.
