### Day27

1. 她有着友好善良的性格。（friendly）
  - She has a friendly and kind personality.
2. 参观博物馆是度过一天的愉快方法。（fun）
  - Visiting the museum is a fun way to spend the day.
3. 除了语文、数学和英语，学生们还要学习政治、历史、地理、物理、化学、生物和其他一些学科。（geography）
  - Besides Chinese, Maths and English, students need to learn Politics, History, Geography, Physics, Chemistry, Biology and some other subjects.
4. 我很高兴能为我们的社区做一些有意义的事。（glad）
  - I am quite glad to do something meaningful for our community.
5. 那是一个值得为之奋斗的目标。（goal）
  - That is a goal worth fighting for.
