### Day62

1. “禁止乱扔垃圾”的标志表明我们不能在公园里扔垃圾。（sign）
  - The sign "No Littering" shows that we must not throw rubbish in the park.
2. 菜单上有一些当地小吃，如炸薯条、饼干和色拉。（snack）
  - There are some local snacks on the menu, such as fried chips, cookies, and salad.
3. 每次读这本书，我总能学到点新的东西。（something）
  - Every time I read this book, I can always learn something new.
4. 她的儿子正在大学学工程学。（son）
  - Her son is studying engineering at university.
5. 它能以大约每小时200英里的速度飞行。（speed）
  - It can fly at a speed of about 200 miles per hour.
