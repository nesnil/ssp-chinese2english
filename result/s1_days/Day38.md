### Day38

1. 你在集市上买了什么？（market）
  - What did you buy at the market?
2. 也许下雨天乘公交车会更好。（maybe）
  - Maybe it's better to take a bus on a rainy day.
3. 听到这令人惊奇的消息我真的很惊讶。（news）
  - I'm really surprised to hear the surprising news.
4. 海浪声总是能让我平静下来。（ocean）
  - The sound of ocean waves always calms me down.
5. 一位警察正在指挥交通。（officer）
  - A police officer is directing the traffic.
