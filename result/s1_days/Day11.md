### Day11

1. 桥被洪水冲走了。（carry）
  - The bridge was carried away by the flood.
2. 他把融化的奶酪三明治拿出了烤箱。（cheese）
  - He takes the melted cheese sandwich out of the oven.
3. 下棋是我最喜欢的爱好。（chess）
  - Playing chess is my favourite hobby.
4. 有些人被迫在家庭和事业之间做出选择。（choice）
  - Some people are forced to make a choice between family and career.
5. 您能递给我一双筷子吗？（chopsticks）
  - Could you please pass me a pair of chopsticks?
