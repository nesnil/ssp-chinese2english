### Day25

1. 这很难用几句话来回答。（few）
  - It is difficult to answer in a few words.
2. 该团队为可再生（renewable）能源领域做出了巨大贡献。（field）
  - This team made great contributions to the field of renewable energy.
3. 经过几天的旅行，他们终于到达了目的地。（finally）
  - After days of traveling, they finally reached their destination.
4. 毕业后，我选择与两位朋友合租一套公寓。（flat）
  - I chose to share a flat with two friends after graduation.
5. 地球上的森林总覆盖量正在减少。（forest）
  - The total forest cover of the Earth is decreasing.
