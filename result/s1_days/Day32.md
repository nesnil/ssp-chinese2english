### Day32

1. 我最好的朋友的积极态度对我产生了很大的影响。（influence）
  - My best friend's positive attitude has a great influence on me.
2. 拼写错误都用红笔圈了出来。（ink）
  - Spelling mistakes are circled in red ink.
3. 与其在房间里看电视，不如我们去公园吧。（instead）
  - Let's go to the park instead of watching TV in the room.
4. 她对音乐有着浓厚的兴趣。（interest）
  - She has a great interest in music.
5. 我家离医院大约2公里。（kilometre）
  - My home is about 2 kilometres from the hospital.
