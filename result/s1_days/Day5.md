### Day5

1. 他扭伤了脚踝，失去了平衡。（balance）
  - He twisted his ankle and lost his balance.
2. 我们用许多五颜六色的气球装饰了生日派对。（balloon）
  - We decorated the birthday party with many colorful balloons.
3. 我认为篮球比棒球更有趣。（basketball）
  - I think basketball is more interesting than baseball.
4. 吃牛肉对你的健康有益。（beef）
  - Eating beef is good for your health.
5. 她从未在她的任何一场比赛中被击败过。（beat）
  - She has never been beaten in any of her games.
