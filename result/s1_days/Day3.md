### Day3

1. 好学生不怕犯错误。（afraid）
  - Good students are not afraid of making mistakes.
2. 你是支持还是反对这项计划？（against）
  - Are you for or against the plan?
3. 我们觉得这是一项惊人的成就。（amazing）
  - We think it's an amazing achievement.
4. 你愿意带我参观一下图书馆吗？（around）
  - Would you like to show me around the library?
5. 当你在罗马，就要像罗马人一样做事。/（入乡随俗。）（as）
  - When you are in Rome, do as the Romans do.
