### Day23

1. 请原谅，但有件事我必须要说一下。（excuse）
  - Please excuse me, but there is something I must say.
2. 电脑太贵了，我买不起。（expensive）
  - The computer is so expensive that I can't afford it.
3. 老师在实验室进行了一次化学实验。（experiment）
  - The teacher performed a chemistry experiment in the laboratory.
4. 不要站在冰上，不然你可能会掉进水里。（fall）
  - Don't stand on the ice, or you may fall into the water.
5. 我们需要更多的食物来养活世界上不断增长的人口。（feed）
  - We need more food to feed the world's growing population.
