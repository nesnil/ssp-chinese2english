### Day6

1. 距离春节只有三个月了。（away）
  - The Spring Festival is only three months away.
2. 河岸边是一个野餐的舒适地点。（bank）
  - The river bank is a comfortable place for a picnic.
3. 你能从浴室的窗户看到花园。（bathroom）
  - You can see the garden from the window of the bathroom.
4. 孩子们正在楼上的卧室里睡觉。（bedroom）
  - The children are sleeping in the bedroom upstairs.
5. 如今，人们对人工智能（AI）的态度变得越来越积极。（become）
