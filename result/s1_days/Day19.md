### Day19

1. 我从一个美国朋友那里学会了做几道菜。（dish）
  - I've learned to cook a few dishes from an American friend.
2. 开车到最近的城镇只需要半个小时。（drive）
  - It only takes half an hour to drive to the nearest town.
3. 专家在会议期间提供了有价值的信息。（during）
  - The expert provided valuable information during the conference.
4. 每个人都有自己的独特品质。（each）
  - Each person has their own unique qualities.
5. 我们应该对老人表示尊敬。（elder）
  - We should show our respect to the elder people.
