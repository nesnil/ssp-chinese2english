### Day8

1. 确保在驾驶时系好你的安全带。（belt）
  - Make sure you fasten your seat belt while driving.
2. 血检表明这个病人已经康复了。（blood）
  - The blood test shows that the patient has recovered.
3. 旅客们正在机场等候登机。（board）
  - Passengers are waiting to board at the airport.
4. 他俩都有足够的勇气在舞台上表演。（both）
  - Both of them are brave enough to perform on the stage.
5. 当你紧张的时候，你可以深呼吸放松一下。（breath）
  - When you are nervous, you can take a deep breath to relax.
