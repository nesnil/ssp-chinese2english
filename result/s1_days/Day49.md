### Day49

1. 她咬了一口多汁的梨。（pear）
  - She took a bite of a juicy pear.
2. 他们计划本周末装饰新房。（plan）
  - They plan to decorate their new house this weekend.
3. 种花给我带来了很大的乐趣。（pleasure）
  - It gives me great pleasure to grow flowers.
4. 我们有足够的时间赶上飞机。（plenty）
  - We have plenty of time to catch the plane.
5. 侦探的讲话简短，但却都切中要点。（point）
  - The detective's speech was short, but all to the point.
