### Day35

1. 你的夹克和蓝色牛仔裤很配。（jacket）
  - Your jacket matches the blue jeans.
2. 我想邀请你加入我们的乐队。（join）
  - I'd like to invite you to join our band.
3. 让她高兴的是，她找到了丢失的裙子。（joy）
  - To her joy, she found her missing skirt.
4. 老人只是想让孩子们和他待在一起。（just）
  - The old man just wants his children to stay with him.
5. 把球踢到马路上是非常危险的。（kick）
  - It's very dangerous to kick the ball into the road.
