### Day10

1. 前方有一个光明的未来正等着我们。（bright）
  - There is a bright future ahead waiting for us.
2. 蜡烛不能两头点。/（不要过分消耗精力。）（burn）
  - Don't burn the candle at both ends.
3. 我们在房间里放了个摄像头照看婴儿。（camera）
  - We put a camera in the room to keep an eye on the baby.
4. 上个假期我参加了一个冬令营。（camp）
  - I took part in a winter camp last vacation.
5. 欲了解更多信息，请联系服务中心。（centre）
  - For more information, please contact the service centre.
