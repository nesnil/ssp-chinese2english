### Day64

1. 故乡在我们脑海中留下了很多甜蜜的回忆。（sweet）
  - Our hometown has left many sweet memories in our minds.
2. 小时候妈妈教我骑自行车。（teach）
  - My mother taught me how to ride a bike when I was young.
3. 没有团队精神，我们的排球队不可能取得现在的胜利。（team）
  - Without team spirit, our volleyball team wouldn't be able to achieve the success it has.
4. 人脸识别技术在如今已广泛使用。（technology）
  - Facial recognition technology is widely used today.
5. 他和他的朋友们每天放学后都会练习打网球。（tennis）
  - He and his friends practice playing tennis after school every day.
