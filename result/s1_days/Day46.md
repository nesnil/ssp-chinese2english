### Day46

1. 父母有责任保护孩子免受伤害。（protect）
  - Parents have a responsibility to protect their children from harm.
2. 能成为队中的一员我感到十分自豪。（proud）
  - I feel very proud to be a part of the team.
3. 我喜欢在上班路上收听早间广播节目。（radio）
  - I enjoy listening to the morning radio show on my way to work.
4. 中国的高铁技术正领先世界。（railway）
  - China's high-speed railway technology is leading the world.
5. 这张老照片让她想起了他们一起度过的快乐时光。（remind）
  - The old photo reminds her of the happy times they had spent together.
