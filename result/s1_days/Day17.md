### Day17

1. 请在到期日期前把书还给图书馆。（date）
  - Please return the books to the library before the due date.
2. 我女儿上学期在学习上取得了很大的进步。（daughter）
  - My daughter made great progress in her studies last term.
3. 写日记是一个提高你写作技巧的好方法。（diary）
  - Keeping a diary is a good way to improve your writing skills.
4. 均衡饮食可以增强你的免疫系统。（diet）
  - Eating a balanced diet can strengthen your immune system.
5. 老师们的话对男孩的人生产生了很大的影响。（difference）
  - Teachers' words made a great difference to the boy's life.
