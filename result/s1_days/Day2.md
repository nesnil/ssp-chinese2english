### Day2

1. 这部动画片适合5岁或以上的孩子。（above）
  - This cartoon is for kids aged 5 or above.
2. 我成为一名演员的梦想将会实现。（actor/actress）
  - My dream of becoming an actor (actress) will come true.
3. 请把你的地址和电话号码写下来。（address）
  - Please write down your address and phone number.
4. 房间里有一个火警警报器。（alarm）
  - There is a fire alarm in the room.
5. 虽然他已经不在人世，但是他的精神永存。（alive）
  - Although he is no longer alive, his spirit lives on.
