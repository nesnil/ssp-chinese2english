### Day1

1. 冰箱里有一些胡萝卜。（carrot）
  - There are a few carrots in the fridge.
2. 实验室从早上8点开放到晚上10点。（a.m.）
  - The lab is open from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m.
3. 她是我们班最活跃的学生之一。（active）
  - She is one of the most active students in our class.
4. 你最喜欢的户外活动是什么？（activity）
  - What's your favourite outdoor activity?
5. 6加4是10。（add）
  - Six and four add up to ten.
