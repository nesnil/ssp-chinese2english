### Day67

1. 这位著名作家创造了很多令人难忘的角色。（unforgettable）
  - The famous writer created many unforgettable characters.
2. 我经常骑自行车去上学。（usually）
  - I usually go to school by bike.
3. 不要从单一的角度看事情。（view）
  - Don't look at things from a single point of view.
4. 我感到非常自豪能成为学校排球队的一员。（volleyball）
  - I feel very proud to be a member of the school volleyball team.
5. 这个网站提供有关我们产品和服务的信息。（website）
  - This website provides information about our products and services.
