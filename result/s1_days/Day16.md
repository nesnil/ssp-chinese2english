### Day16

1. 你去还是不去，由你决定。（decide）
  - It's up to you to decide whether you go or not.
2. 为了表达深深的感激之情，学生们向老师们献上了鲜花。（deep）
  - To show deep gratitude, the students presented flowers to the teachers.
3. 邮递员的职责之一是递送信件和报纸。（deliver）
  - One of a postman's duties is to deliver letters and newspapers.
4. 明天我们是否会去野营要看天气。（depend）
  - Whether we will go camping tomorrow depends on the weather.
5. 在产品设计的过程中，了解目标客户的需求是很重要的。（design）
  - In the process of product design, it's important to understand the target customers' needs.
