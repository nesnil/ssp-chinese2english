### Day59

1. 每个农历十五那天，月亮看上去又圆又亮。（round）
  - The moon looks round and bright on the 15th day of each lunar month.
2. 在消防员的帮助下，我们学到了一些消防安全技巧。（safety）
  - With the help of the firemen, we learned some fire safety skills.
3. 请在汤里加两勺盐。（salt）
  - Please add two spoons of salt to the soup.
4. 我们应该从失败中吸取教训，这样就不会犯同样的错误（/重蹈覆辙）。（same）
  - We should learn from our failures so that we won't make the same mistakes.
5. 他在比赛结束前的最后两分钟进了一球。（score）
  - He scored a goal in the last two minutes before the game was over.
