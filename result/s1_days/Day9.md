### Day9

1. 她天生就是当舞者的料。（born）
  - She was born to be a dancer.
2. 昨天玻璃碗被摔（smash）成了碎片。（bowl）
  - The glass bowl was smashed to pieces yesterday.
3. 让我们休息几分钟。（break）
  - Let's have a few minutes' break.
4. 卷心菜是一种健康的食物，常用于做沙拉。（cabbage）
  - Cabbage is a healthy food that is often used in salads.
5. 他们为无家可归的人提供照顾和服务。（care）
  - They provide care and services for homeless people.
