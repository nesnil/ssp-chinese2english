### Day63

1. 你通常什么时候开始做作业？（start）
  - When do you usually start doing your homework?
2. 给我点一杯草莓奶昔。（strawberry）
  - Order me a strawberry milkshake.
3. 背包旅行是一种流行的旅行方式。（style）
  - Backpacking is a popular style of traveling.
4. 北京是一座如此美丽的城市，我想再去那里。（such）
  - Beijing is such a beautiful city that I want to visit it again.
5. 你可以想象我看到礼物时有多么惊讶。（surprised）
  - You can imagine how surprised I was when I saw the gift.
