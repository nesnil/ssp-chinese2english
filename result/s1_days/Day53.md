### Day53

1. 石油价格增加了百分之十。（price）
  - The price of oil increased by ten percent.
2. 许多国家现在都面临着空气污染的问题。（problem）
  - Many countries are now facing the problem of air pollution.
3. 这款新产品已被消费者广泛接受。（product）
  - The new product has been widely accepted by consumers.
4. 我帮忙把男孩从水里拉了出来。（pull）
  - I helped pull the boy out of the water.
5. 学得快的人更容易教。（quick）
  - Quick learners are easier to teach.
