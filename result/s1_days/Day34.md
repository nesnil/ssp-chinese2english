### Day34

1. 天气预报说稍后会下雨。（later）
  - The weather forecast says it will rain later.
2. 你能测量一下这张桌子的长度吗？（length）
  - Can you measure the length of this table?
3. 新的购物中心位于市中心。（locate）
  - The new shopping mall is located in the city centre.
4. 你有这把锁的钥匙吗？（lock）
  - Have you got the key to this lock?
5. 幸运的是，没有人在车祸中受重伤。（luckily）
  - Luckily, no one was seriously hurt in the car accident.
