### Day1

1. 乘坐公共交通上学的学生占我们班总人数的70%。 (account)
  - Students who take public transport to school account for 70% of the students in our class.
2. 跑完800米后，我浑身都疼，几乎走不动路。 (ache)
  - After running 800 metres, I ached all over and could hardly walk.
3. 这位年轻演员的演技令人印象深刻，为他赢得了大量掌声。 (acting)
  - The young actor's acting skills are impressive, earning him a lot of applause.
4. 这个家庭太贫困了，以至于负担不起孩子的教育费用。 (afford)
  - The family is too poor to afford the child's education expenses.
5. 她跑得很快，始终领先于其他选手。 (ahead)
  - She ran very fast and always stayed ahead of the other runners.

### Day2

1. 虽然这位女演员很有名，但她始终保持谦逊，尊重善待每一个人。 (actress)
  - Although she is famous, the actress always remains humble and treats everyone with kindness and respect.
2. 我们讨论了人工智能是否会改变人们未来的工作和学习方式。 (AI)
  - We discussed whether AI would change the way people work and study in the future.
3. 这项新政策旨在改善空气质量并鼓励人们使用公共交通。 (aim)
  - The new policy is aimed at improving air quality and encouraging people to use public transport.
4. 父母们惊讶于孩子们在夏令营期间成长了不少。 (amazed)
  - The parents were amazed at how much their kids had grown during the summer camp.
5. 如有任何问题，你可以询问任何穿制服的人，他们都准备好为你提供帮助。 (anyone)
  - If you have any questions, you can ask anyone in uniform, and they will be ready to help you.

### Day3

1. 这座城市有很多保存完好的古建筑，其中一些可追溯到几个世纪前。 (ancient)
  - There are many well-preserved ancient buildings in this city, and some of them date back centuries.
2. 我认为在这么短的时间内，没有别人能比她做得更好了。 (anybody)
  - I don't think anybody else could have done a better job than her in such a short time.
3. 总之，只要坚持下来，你可以按自己的方式安排学习计划。 (anyway)
  - Anyway, you can organise your study plan the way you like, as long as you stick to it.
4. 我们应该给那些在社区中表现出善意和责任感的人颁发奖项。 (award)
  - We should give an award to those who show kindness and responsibility in our community.
5. 开设银行账户是青少年学习个人理财的第一步。 (bank)
  - Opening a bank account is the first step for teenagers to learn about personal finance.

### Day4

1. 有了互联网，我们几乎可以在任何地方与家人和朋友保持联系。 (anywhere)
  - With the Internet, we can keep in touch with family and friends from almost anywhere.
2. 这座城市的公寓楼不仅现代化，而且环保，每个屋顶都装有太阳能板。 (apartment)
  - The apartment buildings in this city are not only modern but also eco-friendly, with solar panels on every roof.
3. 许多人认为这位艺术家的作品充满创意和想象力。 (artist)
  - Many people think the works of this artist are full of creativity and imagination.
4. 我们应该给每个学生一个平等的机会来展示他们的才华。 (chance)
  - We should give every student an equal chance to show their talents.
5. 博物馆展出了各朝代的经典画作，让参观者得以体验中国艺术的悠久历史。 (classical)
  - The museum displayed classical paintings from different dynasties, allowing visitors to experience the long history of Chinese art.

### Day5

1. 在餐厅里他总是主动为朋友们买单。 (bill)
  - He always offers to pay the bill for his friends in the restaurant.
2. 有了清晰的计划，我们得以在预算内按时完成项目。 (budget)
  - With a clear plan, we were able to complete the project within budget and on time.
3. 如果气温降到零度以下，滑冰活动可能会出于安全考虑而被取消。 (cancel)
  - If the temperature drops below zero, the ice-skating event may be cancelled for safety reasons.
4. 我们体贴的老师总是课后留下来，帮助那些作业有困难的学生。 (caring)
  - Our caring teacher always stays after class to help students who have difficulty with their homework.
5. 朋友间出现分歧时，倾听彼此并找到解决办法是很重要的。 (case)
  - In the case of a disagreement between friends, it's important to listen to each other and find a solution.
