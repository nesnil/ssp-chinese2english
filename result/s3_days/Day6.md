### Day6

1. 这两种动物在外表上有很多共同之处。 (common)
  - These two animals have a lot in common in appearance.
2. 这款新软件让你更容易掌控你的数字生活。 (control)
  - The new software makes it easier to take control of your digital life.
3. 购买当地人制作的手工制品有助于创造就业机会。 (create)
  - Buying handmade products created by local people can help create jobs.
4. 可爱的小猫太困了，以至于它很快就睡着了。 (cute)
  - The cute kitten was so sleepy that it fell asleep quickly.
5. 由于医疗的改善，那座城市的死亡率已经下降了。 (death)
  - The death rate in that city has decreased due to improved medical care.
