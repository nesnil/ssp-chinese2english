### Day28

1. 校规要求学生们按时到校 (require)
  - The school rules require students to arrive at school on time.
2. 尊重不同的文化和传统是很重要的 (respect)
  - It's important to show respect for different cultures and traditions.
3. 家长应该教会孩子对自己的东西有责任感 。 (responsibility)
  - Parents should teach their children to have a sense of responsibility towards their own things.
4. 你已经开车几个小时了。为什么你不在下一个服务站休息一下呢? (rest)
  - You've been driving for hours. Why don't you take a rest at the next service station?
5. 由于粗心大意，他在考试中犯了很多错误。 (result)
  - As a result of his carelessness, he made a lot of mistakes in the exam.
