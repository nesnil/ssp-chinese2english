### Day35

1. 遵守我们国家的法律是每个公民的义务。 (nation)
  - It's every citizen's duty to obey the laws of our nation.
2. 地震、洪水等自然灾害常常造成巨大破坏。 (natural)
  - Natural disasters like earthquakes and floods often cause great damage.
3. 无论是下雨还是寒冷的天气都无法阻止我们去徒步旅行。 (nor)
  - Neither the rain nor the cold weather could stop us from going hiking.
4. 现如今，越来越多的人喜欢在网上购物。 (nowadays)
  - Nowadays, more and more people prefer shopping online.
5. 学生们被要求将一个与他们的文化相关的物品带到学校。 (object)
  - The students were asked to bring an object related to their culture to school.
