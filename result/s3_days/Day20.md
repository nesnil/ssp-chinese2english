### Day20

1. 科学家们已开发出了一种测量海洋深度的新方法。 (method)
  - Scientists have developed a new method of measuring the depth of the ocean.
2. 千里之行，始于足下。(mile)
  - A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.
3. 经过长时间的思考，她最终下定决心参加比赛。 (mind)
  - After thinking for a long time, she finally made up her mind to take part in the competition.
4. 好奇的猫伸长脖子想看看盒子里有什么 (neck)
  - The curious cat stretched its neck to see what was inside the box.
5. 这条珍贵的项链已代代相传了一个多世纪。 (necklace)
  - This precious necklace has been handed down from generation to generation for over a century.
