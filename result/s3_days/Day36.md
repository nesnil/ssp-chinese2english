### Day36

1. 他们惊讶地看到海滩上如此美丽的日落。 (surprised)
  - They were surprised to see such a beautiful sunset on the beach.
2. 我不小心把手机落在家里，错过了几条重要的短信。 (text)
  - I accidentally left my phone at home and missed several important text messages.
3. 我以为这个考试很容易，但结果却相当困难。 (thought)
  - I thought this exam would be easy, but it turned out to be quite difficult.
4. 考试期间，学生们应该再次检查每个答案，以避免错误。 (through)
  - During the exam, students should go through each answer again to avoid mistakes.
5. 科学家们发现了一种从未见过的微小物种。 (tiny)
  - The scientists discovered a tiny species that had never been seen before.
