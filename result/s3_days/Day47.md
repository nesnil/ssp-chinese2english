### Day47

1. 为了按时完成任务，提前计划并管理好时间是必要的。 (task)
  - To complete a task on time, it's necessary to plan ahead and manage your time well.
2. 团队合作有助于在团队成员之间建立信任和理解。 (teamwork)
  - Teamwork helps build trust and understanding among group members.
3. 我为期末考试努力学习了，所以我以高分通过了考试。 (therefore)
  - I studied hard for the final exams. Therefore, I passed the exams with high scores.
4. 口渴的人们聚集在井边取水，因为已经好几周没有下雨。 (thirsty)
  - The thirsty people gathered around the well to get water because it had not rained for weeks.
5. 尽管我们住在不同的城市，但我们仍然通过微信保持联系。 (touch)
  - Even though we live in different cities, we still keep in touch with each other through WeChat.
