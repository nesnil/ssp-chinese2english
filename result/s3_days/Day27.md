### Day27

1. 稍微等一下，我正要解释关键点。 (hold)
  - Hold on for a moment, I'm about to explain the key point.
2. 渔网上的洞太小，以至于鱼游不过去。 (hole)
  - The holes in the fishing net are too small for the fish to swim through.
3. 他们一回到家就开始做家务。 (housework)
  - They started to do the housework as soon as they got home.
4. 他们把餐费算包括进了账单里。 (include)
  - They included the cost of the meal in the bill.
5. 慈善组织为贫困地区提供资金来发展当地工业。 (industry)
  - The charity organization provides poor areas with money to develop their local industries.
