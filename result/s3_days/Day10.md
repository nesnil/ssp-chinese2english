### Day10

1. 目前尚不清楚新的安全标准何时生效。 (effect)

2. 他在政治生涯中树立了很多强大的敌人。 (enemy)
  - He made many powerful enemies during his political career.
3. 别再找借口了，开始对自己的行为负责吧。 (excuse)
  - Stop making excuses and start taking responsibility for your actions.
4. 我们需要加快速度，尽快完成隧道的建设。 (finish)
  - We need to speed up to finish building the tunnel as soon as possible.
5. 人们保持健康的最佳方法之一是养成健康的饮食习惯。 (fit)
  - One of the best ways for people to keep fit is to develop healthy eating habits.
