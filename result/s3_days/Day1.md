### Day1

1. 古人能够根据太阳的位置分辨时间。 (able)
  - Ancient people were able to tell time by the position of the sun.
2. 经理缺席了会议，所以我们不得不重新安排时间。 (absent)
  - The manager was absent from the meeting, so we had to reschedule it.
3. 公司计划利用新技术来提高生产力。 (advantage)
  - The company plans to take advantage of the new technology to increase productivity.
4. 我同意她在这个问题上的看法。 (agree)
  - I agree with her opinion on this issue.
5. 在未来，人工智能将在我们日常生活中扮演重要角色。 (AI)
  - In the future, AI will play an important role in our daily lives.
