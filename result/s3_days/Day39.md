### Day39

1. 医生建议病人应该尽快休息。 (possible)
  - The doctor suggested that the patient should rest as soon as possible.
2. 当火锅开始沸腾时，我们可以开始加食材了。 (pot)
  - We can start adding ingredients when the hot pot starts to boil.
3. 在把牛奶倒进玻璃杯之前，确保杯子是干净的。 (pour)
  - Before pouring the milk into the glass, make sure the glass is clean.
4. 如果你在火车上遇到紧急情况，请按下按钮寻求帮助。 (press)
  - If you encounter an emergency on the train, please press the button to call for assistance.
5. 我的小学时光充满了欢声笑语，使得它们令人难忘。 (primary)
  - My primary school days were filled with laughter, making them unforgettable.
