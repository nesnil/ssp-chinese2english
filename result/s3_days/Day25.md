### Day25

1. 汉堡包是美国快餐文化中最受欢迎的食物之一。 (hamburger)
  - Hamburgers are one of the most popular foods in American fast-food culture.
2. 魔术师把手伸进他的帽子里，掏出一只鸽子放飞到空中。 (hat)
  - The magician reached into his hat, pulled out a dove, and set it free into the sky.
3. 更好的公共卫生可以极大地提升人们的生活质量。 (health)
  - Better public health can greatly improve the quality of people's lives.
4. 信中隐藏的信息只有少数人才能理解。 (hidden)
  - The hidden message in the letter can only be understood by a few people.
5. 不要试图逃避你的责任。你必须认真对待它们。 (hide)
  - Don't try to hide from your responsibilities. You must take them seriously.
