### Day2

1. 根据物理定律，能量不能被创造或破坏。 (according to)
  - According to physical law, energy cannot be created or destroyed.
2. 这位作家以写情节丰富的小说而闻名。 (action)
  - The author is known for writing novels that are full of action.
3. 这位年轻人决定参军保卫祖国。 (army)
  - The young man decided to join the army to defend his country.
4. 人们应该意识到保护环境的重要性。 (aware)
  - People should be aware of the importance of protecting the environment.
5. 竹筏在古代被用于交通运输。 (bamboo)
  - Bamboo rafts were used for transportation in ancient times.
