### Day22

1. 奥运会每四年举办一次，吸引了来自世界各地的运动员。 (Olympic)
  - The Olympic Games are held every four years, attracting athletes from all over the world.
2. 在开幕式期间，表演者们上演了一场精彩的表演。 (opening)
  - During the opening ceremony, the performers put on a wonderful show.
3. 该软件允许你远程操作系统。 (operate)
  - This software allows you to operate the system remotely.
4. 在我看来，从错误中学习是成长的关键。 (opinion)
  - In my opinion, learning from mistakes is the key to growth.
5. 电梯又出故障了，我们不得不走楼梯。 (order)
  - The lift is out of order again, so we have to take the stairs.
