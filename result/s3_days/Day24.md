### Day24

1. 当他听到坏消息时，他脸色变得苍白。 (pale)
  - When he heard the bad news, his face turned pale.
2. 父母健康的人格对孩子有积极的影响。 (personality)
  - Parents' healthy personalities have a positive influence on children.
3. 教授指出，这个研究课题还没有被探索过。 (point)
  - The professor pointed out that this research topic had not been explored yet.
4. 工厂正在为节日印制大量海报。 (print)
  - The factory is printing a large number of posters for the festival.
5. 在周末，我更喜欢待在家里看喜剧节目。 (programme)
  - On weekends, I prefer to stay at home and watch comedy programmes.
