### Day49

1. 这个装置单元由一个自动运行的计算机程序控制。 (unit)
  - This unit is controlled by a computer program that runs automatically.
2. 机器人不会移动，除非它接收到信号。 (unless)
  - The robot will not move unless it receives a signal.
3. 胜利庆祝后，队员接受了当地媒体的采访。 (victory)
  - After the victory celebration, the team members gave interviews to the local media.
4. 流感病毒在学校和办公室等拥挤场所容易传播。 (virus)
  - The flu virus spreads easily in crowded places like schools and offices.
5. 志愿服务是培养社交技能和积累经验的好方法。 (voluntary)
  - Voluntary service is a great way to develop social skills and gain experience.
