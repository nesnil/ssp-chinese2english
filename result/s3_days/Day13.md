### Day13

1. 两国自上个世纪初以来一直在经贸发展方面进行合作。 (cooperate)
  - These two countries have been cooperating in trade and economic development since the beginning of the last century.
2. 假期即将到来，我期待着我们的家庭旅行。 (corner)
  - The holiday is just around the corner, and I'm looking forward to our family trip.
3. 这件衬衫是棉质的，穿起来很舒服。 (cotton)
  - The shirt is made of cotton, which makes it comfortable to wear.
4. 很多艺术家选择在乡村工作以寻找灵感。 (countryside)
  - Many artists choose to work in the countryside for inspiration.
5. 我们花了几天时间探索这座充满活力的城市。 (couple)
  - We spent a couple of days exploring this lively city.
