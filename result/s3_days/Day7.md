### Day7

1. 讲座太无聊了，以至于我忍不住打了哈欠。 (boring)
  - The lecture was so boring that I couldn't help yawning.
2. 为了惊喜派对，我向朋友们借了一些装饰品。 (borrow)
  - I borrowed some decorations from friends for the surprise party.
3. 人脑比我们曾经认为的要复杂得多。 (brain)
  - The human brain is much more complex than we once thought.
4. 这座旧木屋昨晚意外地被烧毁了。 (burn)
  - The old wooden house was accidentally burned down last night.
5. 我父亲的公司上周派他去欧洲出差。 (business)
  - My father's company sent him on business to Europe last week.
