### Day45

1. 小偷在人群中穿行，以避免被注意到。 (snake)
  - The thief snaked his way through the crowd to avoid being noticed.
2. 该软件是专为年轻用户的需求设计的。 (software)
  - This software is designed for the needs of young users.
3. 地图被摊开在地上，这样每个人都能清楚地看到。 (spread)
  - The map was spread out on the floor so that everyone could see it clearly.
4. 在夏令营停留的时光让孩子们有机会了解自然。 (stay)
  - The stay at the summer camp gave the children a chance to learn about nature.
5. 男孩踩在了香蕉皮上，失去平衡并摔倒了。 (step)
  - The boy stepped on the banana peel, lost his balance, and fell down.
