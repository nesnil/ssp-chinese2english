### Day15

1. 结果与我预想的相当不同。 (different)
  - The result is quite different from what I expected.
2. 科学家在实验室发现了新的化学元素。 (discover)
  - Scientists discovered a new chemical element in the lab.
3. 他努力工作，使他的梦想成真。 (dream)
  - He works hard to make his dream come true.
4. 过去几周汽油价格已经降低了。 (drop)
  - The price of gas has dropped over the past few weeks.
5. 干燥的气候使植物难以生长。 (dry)
  - The dry climate makes it difficult for plants to grow.
