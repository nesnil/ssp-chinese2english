### Day45

1. 尊重他人的人通常也会受到他人的尊重。 (person)
  - A person who respects others is usually respected by others.
2. 请捡起垃圾并把它扔进合适的垃圾桶里。 (pick)
  - Please pick up the rubbish and put it into the proper rubbish bin.
3. 出乎我们意料的是，公园里挤满了野餐的人。 (picnic)
  - To our surprise, the park was crowded with people having picnics.
4. 我们的太阳系由包括地球在内的八颗行星组成。 (planet)
  - Our solar system consists of eight planets including Earth.
5. 许多国家正在采取行动减少塑料污染。 (plastic)
  - Many countries are taking action to reduce plastic pollution.
