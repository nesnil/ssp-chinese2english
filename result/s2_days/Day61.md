### Day61

1. 这枚稀有邮票的价格随着时间的推移而上涨。 (stamp)
  - The price of this rare stamp has increased over time.
2. 公交车站提供有关公交线路的信息。 (station)
  - The bus station provides information about bus routes.
3. 为了避开麻烦而撒谎，最终会导致更多麻烦。 (stay)
  - Lying to stay out of trouble can lead to more trouble in the end.
4. 他离实现目标还有很长的路要走。 (still)
  - He still has a long way to go before he achieves his goal.
5. 这家商店对所有产品都有严格的质量控制流程。 (store)
  - The store has a strict quality-control process for all products.
