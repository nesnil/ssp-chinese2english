### Day18

1. 贫富差距正在不断扩大。 (divide)
  - There is a growing divide between the rich and the poor.
2. 请把纸对折后再剪开。 (double)
  - Please double the paper before you cut it.
3. 你可以从官方网站下载最新版本的软件。 (download)
  - You can download the latest version of the software from the official website.
4. 这种药物对治疗头痛非常有效。 (effective)
  - This medicine is very effective in treating headaches.
5. 在紧急情况下保持冷静是很重要的。 (emergency)
  - It is important to stay calm in an emergency.
