### Day57

1. 蛋糕被做成了一个心的形状。 (shape)
  - The cake was made in the shape of a heart.
2. 收容所里生病的动物需要适当的照料。 (sick)
  - The sick animals in the shelter needed proper care.
3. 自从入夏以来，天气一直相当不稳定。 (since)
  - The weather has been quite unstable since the beginning of summer.
4. 礼盒的大小与礼物的大小完全匹配。 (size)
  - The size of the gift box matches the size of the gift perfectly.
5. 我期待在寒假和朋友们一起在湖上滑冰。 (skate)
  - I look forward to skating on the lake with my friends during the winter vacation.
