### Day43

1. 肩膀的疼痛已经困扰我好几天了。 (pain)
  - The pain in my shoulder has been bothering me for days.
2. 我们需要一个可靠的搭档来开展这一重要的研究项目。 (partner)
  - We need a reliable partner for this important research project.
3. 握手是传染疾病的主要方式之一。 (pass)
  - Shaking hands is one of the major ways to pass on diseases.
4. 海关人员检查了旅客的护照和行李。 (passenger)
  - Customs officers checked the passengers' passports and luggage.
5. 这个故事告诫我们，我们可能会为自己的错误决定付出代价。 (pay)
  - The story warns us that we may pay the price for our bad decisions.
