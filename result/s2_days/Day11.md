### Day11

1. 我不小心点击了错误的文件并且打开了它。 (click)
  - I accidentally clicked the wrong file and opened it.
2. 突然，天空布满了乌云。 (cloud)
  - Suddenly, the sky was covered with dark clouds.
3. 我们提早到达，为了在音乐会上找到好位置。 (concert)
  - We arrived early to get good seats for the concert.
4. 我们需要计算票数看看谁赢得了奖品。 (count)
  - We need to count the votes to see who won the prize.
5. 每个国家都有自己的传统和习俗。 (country)
  - Every country has its own traditions and customs.
