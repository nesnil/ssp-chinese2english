### Day25

1. 政府推出了新政策以改善教育。 (government)
  - The government has introduced new policies to improve education.
2. 随着太阳落山，天空逐渐变得更加昏暗。 (grow)
  - As the sun sets, the sky grows darker.
3. 毕业典礼将在学校礼堂举行。 (hall)
  - The graduation ceremony will take place in the school hall.
4. 我讨厌别人在我说话时打断我。 (hate)
  - I hate it when people interrupt me when I'm speaking.
5. 我们要在高温时多喝水以保持凉爽。 (heat)
  - We need to drink plenty of water to stay cool in the heat.
