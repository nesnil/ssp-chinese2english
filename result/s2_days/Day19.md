### Day19

1. 期末考试前夕我感到非常紧张。 (eve)
  - I feel very nervous on the eve of the final exam.
2. 尽管下着雨，我们还是决定去散步。 (even)
  - Even though it was raining, we decided to go for a walk.
3. 这是一个我们永远不会忘记的难忘活动。 (event)
  - It was a memorable event that we will never forget.
4. 很多学生觉得科学实验既令人兴奋又有趣。 (exciting)
  - Many students find science experiments to be exciting and fun.
5. 你认为成功需要什么样的经验? (experience)
  - What kind of experience do you think is necessary for success?
