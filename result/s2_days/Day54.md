### Day54

1. 魔术师在绳子上打了一个结。 (tie)
  - The magician tied a knot in the rope.
2. 杂志封面上的标题吸引了我的注意力。 (title)
  - The title on the cover of the magazine caught my attention.
3. 他上课时很难理解老师说的话。 (trouble)
  - He had trouble understanding what the teacher said in class.
4. 重要的是寻求真相，而非相信谣言。 (truth)
  - It is important to seek the truth rather than accept rumors.
5. 随着年龄的增长，人们遗忘东西是很正常的。 (usual)
  - It's usual for people to forget things as they get older.
