### Day36

1. 一旦你学会了基础知识，你就可以轻松提高你的技能。 (learn)
  - Once you have learned the basics, you can easily improve your skills.
2. 病了一个星期后，这孩子看起来苍白而虚弱。 (pale)
  - The child looked pale and weak after being sick for a week.
3. 公司的利润今年增长了20%。 (percent)
  - The company's profits increased by twenty percent this year.
4. 也许这本书能为你的学习提供一些有用的方法。 (perhaps)
  - Perhaps this book will provide you with some useful ways for your study.
5. 她吃了一片药来缓解头痛。 (pill)
  - She took a pill to relieve her headache.
