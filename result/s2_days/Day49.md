### Day49

1. 这个电视节目很好，让公众养成了阅读的习惯。 (programme)
  - It's a great TV programme that brings the habit of reading back into the public.
2. 为了防止疾病传播，所有公共服务必须关闭。 (public)
  - In order to prevent the illness from spreading, all public services must be shut down.
3. 作者正致力于在今年出版她的第二部小说。 (publish)
  - The author is working on publishing her second novel this year.
4. 这个问题与我们上周解决的问题很相似。 (quite)
  - This problem is quite similar to the one we solved last week.
5. 我们需要在这个问题上达成共识。 (reach)
  - We need to reach a consensus on this issue.
