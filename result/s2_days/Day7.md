### Day7

1. 牙医建议我一天刷三次牙。 (brush)
  - The dentist advised me to brush my teeth three times a day.
2. 地震后，政府为无家可归的人们建造了庇护所。 (build)
  - After the earthquake, the government built shelters for homeless people.
3. 故宫 (The Forbidden City) 是中国最重要的历史建筑之一。 (building)
  - The Forbidden City is one of the most important historical buildings in China.
4. 看! 这些蝴蝶多漂亮啊! (butterfly)
  - Look! How beautiful these butterflies are!
5. 西安曾经是唐朝 (the Tang Dynasty) 的都城。 (capital)
  - Xi'an was once the capital of the Tang Dynasty.
