### Day16

1. 据报道，由于全球变暖，北极熊正处于危险之中。 (danger)
  - It's reported that polar bears are in danger because of global warming.
2. 在这个地区晚上独自走路是危险的。 (dangerous)
  - It's dangerous to walk alone at night in this area.
3. 一个好的领导者必须能够做出艰难的决定。 (decision)
  - A good leader must be able to make tough decisions.
4. 浪费这么美味的食物真可惜。 (delicious)
  - It's a pity to waste such delicious food.
5. 当她听到坏消息时，她的笑容消失了。 (disappear)
  - Her smile disappeared when she heard the bad news.
