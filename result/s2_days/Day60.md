### Day60

1. 他的建议听起来非常实用，且很有帮助。 (sound)
  - His advice sounds very practical and helpful.
2. 该国南部的气候与北部大不相同。 (south)
  - The climate in the south of this country is quite different from that in the north.
3. 中国每个不同的地区都有自己独特的传统艺术形式。 (special)
  - Every different part of China has its own special forms of traditional art.
4. 熊猫平均每天花12个小时吃竹子。 (spend)
  - Pandas spend an average of 12 hours eating bamboo every day.
5. 广场上的喷泉是游客拍照的热门地点。 (square)
  - The square's fountain is a popular photo spot for tourists.
