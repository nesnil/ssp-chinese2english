### Day13

1. 技术和艺术的结合可以创造更多的可能。 (create)
  - The combination of technology and art can create more possibilities.
2. 学习不同的文化能开阔我们的视野。 (culture)
  - Learning about different cultures can broaden our horizons.
3. 每日锻炼可以改善你的身心健康。 (daily)
  - Daily exercise can improve your physical and mental health.
4. 这个三角形的角度是60度。 (degree)
  - The angle of this triangle is 60 degrees.
5. 如果我们不保护自然，一些野生动物可能会在未来灭绝。 (die)
  - If we don't protect nature, some wildlife may die out in the future.
