### Day4

1. 你知道猎隼 (falcon) 是世界上速度最快的动物之一吗? (among)
  - Do you know that the falcon is among the fastest animals in the world?
2. 如果你去另一个国家，你会买什么东西? (another)
  - If you go to another country, what kind of things would you buy?
3. 有了智能手机，人们可以随时随地发消息。 (anywhere)
  - With smartphones, people can send messages anywhere at any time.
4. 我为我说过的话道歉。 (apologise)
  - I apologise for what I said.
5. 看起来他已经离开了。 (appear)
  - It appears that he has already left.
