### Day29

1. 这座建筑非常巨大，从几英里外就能看到。 (huge)
  - The building is so huge that it can be seen from miles away.
2. 尊重他人的观点很重要，即便我们不同意他们的观点。 (important)
  - It is important to respect other people's opinions, even if we don't agree with them.
3. 为了提高顾客满意度，餐厅改进了服务。 (increase)
  - To increase customer satisfaction, the restaurant improved its service.
4. 请在进入室内游乐场前把鞋脱掉。 (indoor)
  - Please remove your shoes before entering the indoor playground.
5. 让我们在互联网上查找更多有关中国传统艺术的信息。 (information)
  - Let's find out more information about Chinese traditional art on the internet.
