### Day20

1. 冬天的结束意味着春天即将到来。 (end)
  - The end of winter means that spring is on the way.
2. 社交媒体是现在很多人日常生活的一部分。 (everyday)
  - Social media is now a part of many people's everyday lives.
3. 这家餐厅以其优质的服务和美味的食物而闻名。 (excellent)
  - This restaurant is known for its excellent service and delicious food.
4. 当你解释某事时，使用清晰的例子会有所帮助。 (explain)
  - When you explain something, it's helpful to use clear examples.
5. 发烧几天后他开始恢复。 (fever)
  - After a few days of fever, he began to recover.
