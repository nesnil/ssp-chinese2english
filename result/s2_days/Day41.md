### Day41

1. 新闻如何影响人们对社会问题的看法? (news)
  - How does the news affect people's opinions on social issues?
2. 北极以其极端寒冷的气温而闻名。 (north)
  - The North Pole is known for its extreme cold temperatures.
3. 你注意到今天天气变化得有多快吗? (notice)
  - Did you notice how quickly the weather changed today?
4. 关于这个主题已经做了大量的研究。 (number)
  - A large number of studies have been done on this topic.
5. 学校为学生们组织了一次去博物馆的郊游。 (outing)
  - The school organized an outing to the museum for the students.
