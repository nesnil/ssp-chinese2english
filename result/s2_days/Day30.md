### Day30

1. 确保在讲座期间做详细的笔记。 (lecture)
  - Make sure to take detailed notes during the lecture.
2. 如果你把伞借给我，雨一停我就会把它还给你。 (lend)
  - If you lend me your umbrella, I will return it to you as soon as the rain stops.
3. 图书管理员为我的暑期阅读书单推荐了几本小说。 (librarian)
  - The librarian recommended several novels for my summer reading list.
4. 侦探找到了证明嫌疑人说谎的证据。 (lie)
  - The detective found evidence that proved the suspect told a lie.
5. 你能帮我把这个重箱子抬到架子上吗? (lift)
  - Can you help me lift this heavy box onto the shelf?
