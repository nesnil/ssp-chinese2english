### Day48

1. 花瓶里的花闻起来没有昨天那么香了。 (smell)
  - The flowers in the vase don't smell as sweet as they did yesterday.
2. 社交媒体改变了人们相互沟通的方式。 (social)
  - Social media has changed the way people communicate with each other.
3. 柔和的音乐让气氛更加轻松。 (soft)
  - The soft music made the atmosphere more relaxing.
4. 这次演讲太有力量了，每个人都被深深感动。 (speech)
  - The speech was so powerful that everyone was deeply touched.
5. 背景中奇怪的声响打断了他们的谈话。 (strange)
  - The strange noise in the background interrupted their conversation.
