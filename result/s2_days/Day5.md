### Day5

1. 你家乡的平均气温是多少? (average)
  - What is the average temperature in your hometown?
2. 请原谅。我把你当成别人了。 (beg)
  - I beg your pardon. I thought you were someone else.
3. 不要把电池扔进垃圾箱里。 (bin)
  - Don't throw the batteries in the bin.
4. 他不小心打翻了一个墨水瓶。 (bottle)
  - He accidentally knocked over an ink bottle.
5. 如果你违反法律，你将会受到惩罚。 (break)
  - If you break the law, you will be punished.
