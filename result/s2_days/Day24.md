### Day24

1. 我发现当噪音太大时很难集中注意力。 (focus)
  - I find it hard to focus when there is too much noise.
2. 如果你忘记了密码，可以在这里重置。 (forget)
  - If you forget your password, you can reset it here.
3. 她在比赛中的出色表现获得了认可。 (gain)
  - She gained recognition for her outstanding performance in the match.
4. 我的老师有着温和的教学方法，使学习令人愉快。 (gentle)
  - My teacher has a gentle way of teaching that makes learning enjoyable.
5. 社区计划为无家可归者建造更多庇护所。 (homeless)
  - The community plans to build more shelters for the homeless.
