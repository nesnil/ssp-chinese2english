### Day64

1. 该部落保留了其传统的生活方式，依赖狩猎为生。 (traditional)
  - The tribe has preserved its traditional way of life, relying on hunting for sustenance.
2. 上海的交通网络变得越来越方便。 (transport)
  - The transport network in Shanghai has become more and more convenient.
3. 乘地铁是一个节省时间和金钱的好方法。 (underground)
  - Taking the underground is a good way to save time and money.
4. 军装是荣誉和责任的象征。 (uniform)
  - The military uniform is a symbol of honour and duty.
5. 她要等到完成工作才离开。 (until)
  - She won't leave until she finishes her work.
