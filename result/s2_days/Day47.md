### Day47

1. 公司愿意为新员工的发展提供平台。 (platform)
  - The company is willing to provide a platform for the development of new employees.
2. 这首诗描绘了一幅宁静的乡村生活场景。 (poem)
  - The poem describes a peaceful scene of rural life.
3. 政府为贫困地区的学生提供了一些新的计算机设备。 (poor)
  - The government provided students in poor areas with some new computer equipment.
4. 图书馆张贴了有关新借书系统的公告。 (post)
  - The library posted an announcement about the new book-lending system.
5. 发电站为整个城市提供电力。 (power)
  - The power station provides electricity for the whole city.
