### Day39

1. 我不是故意打破花瓶的。 (mean)
  - I didn't mean to break the vase.
2. 这种药的副作用可能很严重。 (medicine)
  - The side effects of this medicine can be serious.
3. 小心不要搞混了你电脑上的文件。 (mix)
  - Be careful not to mix up the files on your computer.
4. 什么样的品质能使某人成为好榜样? (model)
  - What qualities make someone a good role model?
5. 我们街区有很多餐馆可以选择。 (neighbourhood)
  - There are many restaurants in our neighbourhood to choose from.
