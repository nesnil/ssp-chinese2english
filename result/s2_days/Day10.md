### Day10

1. 粗心的学生经常在考试中丢分。 (careless)
  - Careless students often lose marks in exams.
2. 学校组织了一次慈善活动，为有需要的学生募捐。 (charity)
  - The school organized a charity event to raise money for students in need.
3. 我更喜欢面对面聊天，而不是通过短信。 (chat)
  - I prefer to chat face-to-face rather than through text messages.
4. 演出结束后，观众们欢呼鼓掌。 (cheer)
  - After the performance, the audience cheered and applauded.
5. 选择一本好书是提升你阅读技巧的关键。 (choose)
  - Choosing a good book is the key to improving your reading skills.
