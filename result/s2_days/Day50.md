### Day50

1. 我正在睡觉，闹钟突然响了。 (suddenly)
  - I was sleeping when the alarm clock suddenly went off.
2. 很多人在春季会遭受过敏的困扰。 (suffer)
  - Many people suffer from allergies during the spring season.
3. 我不确定我的建议是否对你有帮助。 (suggestion)
  - I am not sure if my suggestion is helpful to you.
4. 地球表面近四分之三被水覆盖。 (surface)
  - Nearly three quarters of the Earth's surface is covered by water.
5. 调查询问了参与者对社交媒体的看法。 (survey)
  - The survey asked participants about their opinions on social media.
