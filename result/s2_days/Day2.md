### Day2

1. 过马路时看手机容易导致事故。 (accident)
  - Looking at the phone while crossing the road may cause an accident.
2. 事实上，现在并没有在下雨。 (actually)
  - Actually, it's not raining now.
3. 这里是一条关于学生如何应对危险的建议。 (advice)
  - Here is a piece of advice for students on how to deal with danger.
4. 我们老师不允许我们在学校使用手机。 (allow)
  - Our teacher doesn't allow us to use mobile phones in the school.
5. 要解决这个问题几乎是不可能的。 (almost)
  - It's almost impossible to solve this problem.
