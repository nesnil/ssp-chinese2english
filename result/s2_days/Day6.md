### Day6

1. 记者写了一篇有关新政策的文章。 (article)
  - The journalist wrote an article about the new policy.
2. 在战争中，攻击和防御同样重要。 (attack)
  - In war, attack and defense are equally important.
3. 台风期间我们应该避免站在树木附近。 (avoid)
  - During a typhoon, we should avoid standing near trees.
4. 你能告诉我如何在工作和娱乐之间保持平衡吗? (balance)
  - Can you tell me how to keep a balance between work and play?
5. 水、食物和空气是生命的基本需求。 (basic)
  - Water, food and air are the basic needs for life.
