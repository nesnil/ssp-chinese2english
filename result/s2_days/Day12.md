### Day12

1. 作家清晰地描述了故事中的每个细节。 (clearly)
  - The writer described every detail in the story clearly.
2. 青少年们应该多与父母沟通。 (communicate)
  - Teenagers should communicate more with their parents.
3. 我能和你一起参加社区服务吗? (community)
  - Can I join you in the community service?
4. 在好朋友的陪伴下，时间过得很快。 (company)
  - In the company of good friends, time flies quickly.
5. 建立自信需要时间和练习。 (confidence)
  - Building confidence takes time and practise.
