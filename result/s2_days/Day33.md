### Day33

1. 我收藏的每一件物品都有自己的历史。 (item)
  - Each item in my collection has its own history.
2. 我上个月买的牛仔裤现在正在打折。 (jeans)
  - The jeans I bought last month are on sale now.
3. 听到这个笑话时，她情不自禁地笑了起来。 (laugh)
  - She couldn't help laughing when she heard the joke.
4. 我要尽快离开去赶飞机。 (leave)
  - I need to leave soon to catch my flight.
5. 因为气候变化，所以海平面正在上升。 (level)
  - The sea level is going up because of climate change.
