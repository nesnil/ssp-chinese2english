### Day58

1. 鸟类的翅膀完美地适应于飞行。 (wing)
  - Birds' wings are perfectly adapted for flight.
2. 成为真正的赢家并不总是意味着成为第一。 (winner)
  - Being a real winner doesn't always mean being the first.
3. 制作乐器的木材必须经过精心挑选。 (wood)
  - The wood used for making musical instruments must be carefully selected.
4. 培养实用的生活技能对学生有益。 (skill)
  - Developing practical life skills is beneficial for students.
5. 皮疹可能由过敏或感染引起。 (skin)
  - Skin rashes can be caused by allergies or infections.
