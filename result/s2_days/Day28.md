### Day28

1. 保险柜的钥匙藏在一本书里。 (key)
  - The key to the safe is hidden in a book.
2. 受污染的空气和水可以杀死植物、动物，甚至人。 (kill)
  - Polluted air and water can kill plants, animals, and even people.
3. 那位女士在会议上发表了精彩的演讲。 (lady)
  - That lady gave a wonderful speech at the conference.
4. 这片土地是很多不同种类植物的家园。 (land)
  - This land is home to many different species of plants.
5. 互联网使得学习新语言变得更加容易。 (language)
  - The internet has made it easier to learn new languages.
