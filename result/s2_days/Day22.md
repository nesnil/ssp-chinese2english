### Day22

1. 房间里充满了刚出炉的面包味道。 (fill)
  - The room is filled with the smell of freshly baked bread.
2. 老师把手指举到嘴边，示意安静。 (finger)
  - The teacher raised her finger to her lips as a sign for silence.
3. 渔民耐心地等待着鱼儿上钩。 (fisherman)
  - The fisherman waited patiently for the fish to bite.
4. 他试图修好电脑，但电脑损坏严重。 (fix)
  - He tried to fix the computer, but it was badly damaged.
5. 流感会在人群密集的地方迅速传播。 (flu)
  - The flu can spread quickly in crowded places.
