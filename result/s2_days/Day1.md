### Day1

1. 每个人生来就有学习的能力。 (ability)
  - Everyone is born with the ability to learn.
2. 许多研究表明，这一行为有很多好处。 (act)
  - Many studies have shown that this act has many benefits.
3. 我希望你能接受我的道歉。 (accept)
  - I hope you can accept my apology.
4. 他匆匆忙忙地跑过马路。 (across)
  - He ran across the road in a hurry.
5. 当我到达机场时，飞机已经起飞了。 (airport)
  - By the time I arrived at the airport, the plane had already taken off.
