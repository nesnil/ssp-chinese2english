### Day4

1. 有了互联网，我们几乎可以在任何地方与家人和朋友保持联系。 (anywhere)
  - With the Internet, we can keep in touch with family and friends from almost anywhere.
2. 这座城市的公寓楼不仅现代化，而且环保，每个屋顶都装有太阳能板。 (apartment)
  - The apartment buildings in this city are not only modern but also eco-friendly, with solar panels on every roof.
3. 许多人认为这位艺术家的作品充满创意和想象力。 (artist)
  - Many people think the works of this artist are full of creativity and imagination.
4. 我们应该给每个学生一个平等的机会来展示他们的才华。 (chance)
  - We should give every student an equal chance to show their talents.
5. 博物馆展出了各朝代的经典画作，让参观者得以体验中国艺术的悠久历史。 (classical)
  - The museum displayed classical paintings from different dynasties, allowing visitors to experience the long history of Chinese art.
